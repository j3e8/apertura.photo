Welcome to Apertura!

We're glad you've joined us in the adventure of organizing and preserving all your photos. To get started, you'll need to download the app on all your devices. Once it's downloaded, you'll run the app, sign in and we'll start collecting all your memories and uploading them to this account. You'll always be able to view them here on your phone, computer or tablet.

Downloads

Windows

Windows 32/64-bit installer v1.5
Windows 7, 8 and 10
  1. Download and run the Windows installer
  2. Locate and run the Apertura Settings program from the Start Menu or in your Program Files directory
  3. Sign in to your Apertura account
  4. Click the Add button to choose folders for Apertura to scan for photos
  5. The app status (at the bottom of the window) should say "Uploading" or "Uploaded". If so, the Apertura service is running in the background, finding and uploading photos. You can close this settings window and forget all about it. During the initial upload, you may want to adjust your settings to prevent your PC from going to sleep or hibernating for a few days.
Version 1.5 now uploads DNG, PEF, ARW, MRW, ORF, RAF and DCR raw camera formats in addition to the already-supported NEF, CRW and CR2 raw formats.

Mac OS X

Mac OS X installer v1.4
Mac OS X 10.8 and higher
  1. Download and double-click the Mac OS X package
  2. Find and run the Apertura Settings app in the Applications folder
  3. Sign in to your Apertura account
  4. Click the Browse button to choose folders for Apertura to scan for photos
  5. The app status (at the bottom of the window) should say "Uploading" or "Uploaded". If so, the Apertura daemon is running in the background, finding and uploading photos. You can close this settings window and forget all about it. Apertura will upload photos whenever the Mac is turned on and not asleep. During the initial upload, you may want to adjust your settings to prevent your Mac from going to sleep for a few days.
Version 1.4 now uploads DNG, PEF, ARW, MRW, ORF, RAF and DCR raw camera formats in addition to the already-supported NEF, CRW and CR2 raw formats.

iOS
  1. Download the iOS app from the App Store and open it
  2. Sign in to your Apertura account
  3. Choose the settings you want to use to upload photos from your device.
Note: Apple devices only permit photos to be uploaded to Apertura while you're using the app. Because of this, it may take several days to sync all the photos from your device to your Apertura account. To get all your photos uploaded faster, you can turn off Auto-Lock in Settings, plug in your device and leave Apertura open overnight. You can check the progress of your uploads from the Settings tab of the app.
