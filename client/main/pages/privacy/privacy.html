We're serious about privacy

We know your photos are important to you and that they're private. It's our policy to maintain complete privacy of our customers' photos. We don't share them or any of your personal information with any third parties--not even advertising networks. We know our service is useful and doesn't require subsidies from ad revenue to keep us happy. Your other personal information, including your credit card information is stored in a secure PCI-compliant facility apart from Apertura and will never be shared either.

By using the site, you do agree to have cookies (small pieces of information such as your user ID) stored on your computer to enable you to quickly access all the features of the site. This is a standard part of visiting most any website and does not compromise your security in any way. You should remember, however, to always sign out when using Apertura on a public computer or someone else's device.
