How it works

Take photos with your camera or phone
Apertura supports photos taken with any device including most RAW camera files.
Apertura finds new photos on your devices, syncs and organizes them
Download Apertura on your phones, tablets and computers. Whenever the app detects new photos it will automatically upload them to your account and organize them by the date they were taken. You can add your own tags to add more organization and make them searchable.
Find photos quickly on any device wherever you are
Use the Apertura app or website to quickly scroll through photos you’ve taken each year, month and day. It’s easy to find photos of holidays and events and more on any device so you can share memories with friends and family or start a photo project.
